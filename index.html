About Me

I am loving learning to code and am committed to a lifelong learning of programming. I am going to keep pushing myself and hope to inspire other women to do the same. I volunteer with Girl Develop It and attend PyLadies meetups to engage other women in the industry. I hope to someday mentor other students who are interested in attending a bootcamp to learn programming. When I'm not learning new coding skills you can often find me hiking the headlands or on a boat whale watching. As an SF Mission native I am also passionate about tacos.
